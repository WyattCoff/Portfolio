This page is to advise what you need enter on the form

The First name field must have your first name entered and must not be blank.

The first and last name field must be entered and can not be left blank.

The email address must be a minimum of 8 characters and contain an “@” and a “.” (dot).

The postcode must contain four numbers and No letters.

The credit Card number must be the full 16 digit number with no spaces and no letters. The credit card month and year must be valid.

The CCV must be entered and must be a 3 digit number from the back of the credit card and have no letters.
